"We didn't open a restaurant — we built a vibe. A place where everyone from Lahore could come, eat something amazing, and leave happy."

FOUR was born from a simple idea: Lahore deserved a premium fast food experience without pretension. Founded in 2023 by content creators Ducky Bhai (Saad Rehman), Iqra Kanwal, Areeb Pervaiz, and Aroob Jatoi — four friends who turned their love of food into one of Pakistan's most exciting restaurant brands.

Located in the heart of DHA Raya, Phase 6, our triple-story restaurant is designed for everyone — families, late-night foodies, date nights, and everything in between. Open daily from 1 PM to 3 AM, we're Lahore's go-to spot when the craving hits.

Every item on our menu is obsessively crafted — from the smashed beef patties to the malai crust pizza. We source quality ingredients, combine them with bold flavours, and serve them fast without cutting corners.
