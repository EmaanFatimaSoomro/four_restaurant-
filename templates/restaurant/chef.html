Career

Training & Accolades

🇫🇷

Paris, France

Apprenticed under three-starred chef Pierre Gagnaire, mastering the discipline of classical French haute cuisine.

🇯🇵

Kyoto, Japan

A transformative year studying kaiseki philosophy — learning restraint, seasonality, and the art of visual harmony.

🇮🇹

Modena, Italy

Worked alongside Massimo Bottura at Osteria Francescana, discovering the power of storytelling through food.

🌟

New York, 2008–Present

Founded Aurum, earning three Michelin stars and international recognition as one of the world's great culinary destinations.
